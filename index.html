Who Are We?

We blend the fun of temporary tattoos with cutting-edge augmented reality (AR) technology to create an unforgettable, interactive experience. Each of our tattoos features bold, cartoon-like designs that spring to life when viewed through your phone. Whether it’s a butterfly taking flight or a vibrant animation lighting up your arm, our tattoos are designed to inspire wonder and awe.

Perfect for kids’ birthday parties, festivals, or any event that calls for a touch of magic, InkVision temporary tattoos are easy to use and fun to share. Simply scan the dedicated QR code for your tattoo, follow the prompts, and watch as the artwork transforms into an amazing AR experience right before your eyes.

At InkVision, we’re pushing the boundaries of creativity and technology to turn temporary tattoos into a living canvas.

Birthday Party Tattoos

How to Enjoy Your Tattoo!

  1. Skin should be clean & dry.
  2. Remove clear top sheet.
  3. Press tattoo, design facing down, onto skin.
  4. Hold wet cloth against back of tattoo.
  5. Wait 10 seconds, then peel off paper backing.
  6. Scan your tattoo's QR code to visit your tattoo's site.
  7. Point your camera at your tattoo.
  8. Watch it come alive!
